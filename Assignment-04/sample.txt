Cricket is a popular sport played and loved by millions across the globe.
It is a game of strategy, skill, and teamwork, requiring players to excel in batting, bowling, and fielding.
The game is played between two teams, each consisting of eleven players, on a circular or oval-shaped field. 
The primary objective is to score more runs than the opposing team while adhering to the rules of the game. 
Cricket has various formats, including Test matches, One-Day Internationals, and T20s, each offering unique challenges and excitement. 
The sport has a rich history, with legendary players leaving an indelible mark on its legacy. 
Fans eagerly follow tournaments like the ICC World Cup and IPL, showcasing thrilling performances. 
Cricket fosters a sense of unity and sportsmanship among players and spectators alike. 
It is not just a game but a passion that brings people together. 
The sound of the bat hitting the ball and the roar of the crowd create unforgettable moments in cricket.
